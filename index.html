FAQs & Quick Tips
Common client questions answered
How do I choose the right sum assured?

Start with income replacement (10-15 years), then add liabilities and goal funding. Use the Need Analysis tool for a quick estimate.

Which documents should I carry for onboarding?

Basic KYC, income proof, age proof, and recent photo are usually enough to start. Keep digital copies ready to share.

Can I share brochures directly?

Yes. Open any brochure and tap Save to download or share via your preferred app.
